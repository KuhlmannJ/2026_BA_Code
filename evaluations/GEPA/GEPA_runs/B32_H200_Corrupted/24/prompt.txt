### Allowed GHG units
Accept only units that are standard GHG reporting units. The unit must appear **exactly** as printed and be attached to the numeric value (optional space). Typical examples:

* `ktCO₂e`  
* `tonnes CO2e`  
* `tonnes CO₂e`  
* `tCO2e`  
* `t CO2e`  

If the unit differs from these patterns, treat the row as non‑GHG‑total and ignore it.

3. **Validate the row** – ensure the unit matches an allowed GHG unit and that the label (if any) does **not** contain percentages, per‑capita, per‑employee, or other normalisation qualifiers.
